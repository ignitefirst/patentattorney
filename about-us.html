About Us

At Project Patent by Kaufhold and Dix Patent Law, we specialize in delivering a holistic suite of intellectual property services that empower inventors, creators, and businesses to harness the full potential of their innovations. Our seasoned patent attorneys, armed with decades of experience and a proven track record of successfully securing thousands of patents and trademarks, are dedicated to guiding clients through the intricacies of the patent process. We prioritize clear communication, ensuring that our clients are well-informed about the various types of patent protections available—utility, design, and plant patents—to tailor our approach to suit each unique invention.

What distinguishes Project Patent is our commitment to transparency and reliability. We offer flat-fee arrangements that cover all necessary legal work, eliminating the uncertainty of hidden costs and enabling our clients, particularly individual inventors and small businesses, to plan their finances confidently. Our clear pricing model reflects our dedication to building lasting relationships grounded in trust and clarity, which is essential for those navigating the complex landscape of intellectual property.

Beyond securing patents and trademarks, we provide comprehensive support throughout the entire journey—from the initial evaluation of an idea's patentability to drafting and submitting applications and following up during examination processes. At Project Patent, we view ourselves as strategic partners for inventors and companies, helping them transform their creative and technical innovations into protected and monetizable assets. Our extensive expertise, commitment to client-centric service, and proven results make us the ideal choice for anyone seeking robust intellectual property protection.
